Get the application URL by running these commands:

    kubectl port-forward service/news-identity-service {{ .Values.service.identity.port}} -n {{ .Chart.Name }}
    curl --location --request POST 'localhost:{{ .Values.service.identity.port }}/api/v1/identity/user/registration' \
    --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
    --data-raw '{
           "username": "user",
           "email": "user@gmail.com",
           "password": "user"
        }'
